Vulnerable Lives 2026

Cohabition, Violence and Differrence

Vulnerability has emerged as one of the most pressing categories of contemporary thought:
not only a marker of fragility, but also a condition of relationality and interdependence.
To think vulnerability is to interrogate how lives are recognised or denied as lives,
how violence shapes the limits of community, and how responsibility
can be articulated in conditions of exposure.
